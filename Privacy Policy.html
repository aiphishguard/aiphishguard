Privacy Policy - AI Phish Guard

Effective Date: January 06, 2026

Last Updated: January 06, 2026

Welcome to AI Phish Guard. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web-based phishing URL detection service at https://aiphishguard.site/.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 URLs You Submit

When you use AI Phish Guard to analyze a URL, we temporarily process the submitted URL for phishing detection using machine learning models, heuristics, and optional third-party reputation checks.

  • Processed in real-time for AI and content analysis
  • Not stored on our servers after analysis
  • Not shared with unauthorized third parties
  • Automatically deleted after the scan completes

1.2 Technical Data

We may collect minimal anonymous technical information to improve service functionality, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., number of scans performed)
  • Error logs (without personal identifiers)

We do not collect IP addresses, personal identifiers, or tracking data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide real-time phishing detection
  • Improve the accuracy and performance of our models
  • Diagnose and resolve technical issues
  • Enhance overall user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

We prioritize your privacy:

  • No Long-Term Storage: Submitted URLs and scan results are not retained
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS with industry-standard TLS
  • No Accounts Required: No login or personal information is needed

4. Third-Party Services

AI Phish Guard may use the following third-party services for enhanced detection:

  • VirusTotal API (optional reputation checks) – Processed according to Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • WHOIS/RDAP Queries (domain information) – Public data only

These services receive only the URL (no personal data) and handle it per their policies.

5. Children's Privacy

AI Phish Guard is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any data we might hold (currently none for individual users)
  • Request deletion (automatic after scans)
  • Opt out of any optional features

7. Data Retention

We retain no submitted URLs or personal data. Anonymous aggregated statistics may be kept indefinitely for service improvement.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date.
